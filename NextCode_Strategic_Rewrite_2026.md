# 4️⃣ Internal-First Strategy ("Cordonnier Bien Chaussé")

## Phase 1 --- Internal Infrastructure

### Hardware Assumption

-   1 DGX Spark ≈ 5,000 €
-   Capacity target: \~30 consultants (medium usage profile)
-   Scaling rule:
    -   30 consultants → 1 machine
    -   60 consultants → 2 machines (N+1 resilience)
    -   100 consultants → 3 machines

### OPEX Estimate per Machine

-   Amortization (36 months): \~139 €/month
-   Electricity (\~300W continuous): \~43 €/month
-   Total infra baseline: \~182 €/month (excluding human cost)

Even with 2 machines: ≈ 364 €/month infra baseline

Compare: Cursor Teams: \~\$40/user/month\
50 users → \~\$2,000/month

Infrastructure is not the expensive part.\
**Product, governance, and security are.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Why Internal First?

1.  Credibility ("we use what we sell")
2.  Measurable ROI: number of projects over time, time for one project, number of people per project
3.  Reduced vendor dependency
4.  Talent attraction
5.  Capitalized engineering knowledge
